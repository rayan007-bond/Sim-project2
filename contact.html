Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services and processes.

How do I schedule a consultation?

You can schedule a consultation by calling our office, filling out the contact form on this page, or emailing us directly. We typically respond within 24 hours to set up an appointment.

What should I bring to my first appointment?

Please bring any relevant documents related to your case, such as contracts, court papers, identification documents, and any correspondence you've had with other parties involved.

What are your billing practices?

We offer various billing arrangements including hourly rates, flat fees for certain services, and contingency fees where appropriate. During your initial consultation, we'll discuss the best billing option for your situation.

Do you offer virtual consultations?

Yes, we offer virtual consultations via video conference for clients who cannot visit our offices in person. This can be arranged when scheduling your appointment.

How long does a typical case take?

The duration of a case varies significantly depending on its complexity, the court's schedule, and whether a settlement is reached. During your consultation, we can provide a more specific timeline based on your particular circumstances.
